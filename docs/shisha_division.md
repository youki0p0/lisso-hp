# Shisha Division

## Positioning

LISSO treats shisha as smokable gastronomy.

The goal is not only to provide sweet, refreshing, or popular flavors, but to design taste through aroma, sweetness, acidity, bitterness, cooling, salinity, body, aftertaste, heat behavior, and time transition.

## Core Philosophy

- Shisha is treated as "吸う料理".
- Taste is designed by theory, not only by feeling.
- A mix should have a beginning, middle, and ending.
- Mint is not always the protagonist; it often completes the structure.
- Olive, green tea, white tea, nettle, and similar flavors may act as infrastructure.
- Salinity is considered a structural concept that sharpens sweetness, acidity, bitterness, and aroma.

## Store Tone

LISSO should feel theoretical, calm, high-quality, and approachable.

Target customers:

- Beginners who want understandable taste
- Cafe lovers
- People interested in unusual flavors
- Advanced shisha users interested in mixology theory
