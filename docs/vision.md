# LISSO Vision

LISSO is a company that designs experiences through taste, aroma, infrastructure, security, and technology.

The company has two primary divisions:

## Shisha Division

The Shisha Division treats shisha as "smokable gastronomy" rather than simple flavored tobacco.

It focuses on:

- Flavor design
- Mixology theory
- Sensory evaluation
- Recipe sharing
- EC-connected purchase guidance
- Store experience

## Technology Division

The Technology Division provides technical support in infrastructure, cyber security, and DX promotion.

It focuses on:

- Infrastructure design and operation
- AWS / Google Cloud security enhancement
- Cloud migration
- DX promotion
- System renewal
- Operational improvement
- SES / technical support

## Unified Concept

LISSO should be presented as:

> A company that designs experiences with theory and precision.

Shisha and technology should not be mixed carelessly, but they should be connected by the shared concept of design, structure, and operation.
