# Claude Design Prompt

Design the entire LISSO web ecosystem.

Do not ask for colors or spacing. Make design decisions autonomously.

## Brand

LISSO is a company that designs experiences.

- Shisha Division: aroma, taste, gastronomy, mixology
- Technology Division: infrastructure, cyber security, DX promotion

## Visual Direction

- Dark mode by default
- Black, white, and antique gold accents
- Spacious layout
- Typography first
- Elegant, calm, high-quality
- Inspired by Apple, Linear, Anthropic, Stripe, and Notion
- Avoid excessive borders and cards
- Avoid cheap luxury
- Mobile first
